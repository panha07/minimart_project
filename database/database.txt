Polymorphism មាន២ 
 - Static Polymorphism (Compile-time Polymorphism) ->  ប្រភេទនៃ Polymorphism​ នេះ achieved ដោយ overloading method
 - Dynamic Polymorphism(Runtime Polymorphism) -> ប្រភេទនៃ Polymorphism​ នេះ achieved ដោយ override method
